Market Trends Analysis

How have luxury housing bookings changed quarter by quarter across micro-markets?

  • Whitefield dominates with 1,200+ bookings in Q3 2024 - highest among all micro-markets
  • Sarjapur Road growth: Bookings increased from 380 (Q1 2023) to 520 (Q3 2024)
